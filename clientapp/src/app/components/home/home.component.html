File-ripper is a lightweight library for tearing into your text files and unleashing the data inside. With file-ripper, you're in control of reading your files. You define the format of your files via file-ripper's convenient FileDefinition and FieldDefinition interfaces. You decide how to persist your file configurations for use, and you decide how to use your data once it is returned. File-ripper supports fixed-width, character delimited, and XML formats. Your data is returned in a simple format that can easily be converted to JSON for submitting to a REST API. Optionally, you can specify an object builder function to have your data returned in the data type of your choice.
